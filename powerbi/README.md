
# Prime Video Dashboard – Power BI

## 📊 Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard provides an analytical overview of content available on **Prime Video**.
It visualizes information such as total titles, genres, ratings, release years, and country distribution of movies and TV shows.

The dashboard helps users quickly understand the structure of Prime Video's content library and identify trends in content production and classification.

---

## 📁 Dataset Information

The dataset contains metadata about titles available on Prime Video.

Key attributes in the dataset include:

* **Title** – Name of the movie or TV show
* **Type** – Movie or TV Show
* **Director** – Director of the title
* **Country** – Country of production
* **Release Year** – Year the content was released
* **Rating** – Audience rating category (e.g., 13+, 16+, PG-13)
* **Genre** – Category of the content
* **Date Added** – When the title was added to Prime Video

---

## 📌 Dashboard Features

### 1️⃣ Summary KPIs

The top section displays key metrics:

* **Total Titles:** 9,655
* **Total Ratings:** 25
* **Total Genres:** 519
* **Total Directors:** 5,771
* **Start Date:** 1920
* **End Date:** 2021

These KPIs provide a quick overview of the dataset scale.

---

### 2️⃣ Ratings by Total Shows

A horizontal bar chart showing the number of titles by **content rating category**, such as:

* 13+
* 16+
* ALL
* 18+
* R
* PG-13
* 7+

This helps identify the dominant content rating categories on Prime Video.

---

### 3️⃣ Genres by Total Shows

Displays the most common genres available on the platform.

Top genres include:

* Drama
* Comedy
* Drama & Suspense
* Comedy & Drama
* Animation & Kids
* Documentary
* Kids

This chart highlights the diversity of content categories.

---

### 4️⃣ Total Shows by Country

A geographic map visualizing the **distribution of titles by country**.

It helps identify which countries contribute the most content to Prime Video.

---

### 5️⃣ Movies vs TV Shows

A donut chart comparing the proportion of:

* **Movies:** 7.81K (≈80.82%)
* **TV Shows:** 1.85K (≈19.18%)

This shows that movies dominate the platform’s catalog.

---

### 6️⃣ Total Shows by Release Year

A line chart showing the number of titles released each year.

Key insights:

* Minimal content before the 1980s
* Rapid growth after 2000
* Significant spike after 2015

This reflects the growth of streaming-era content production.
